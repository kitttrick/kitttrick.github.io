About Me

My name is Katy and I am a Junior Software Developer, based in Guildford, Surrey, England.

Up until this year (2024) I was working as a Payroll Manager in a software and cyber security company based in Surrey. I have always been very interested in anything related to technology, and I was offered an incredible opportunity with my employer to transition over to the development team from the payroll team. I had very limited experience in programming so this was a huge leap of faith for the company, and I couldn't be more happpy that I was given the opportunity. I have been learning how to be a software developer with Codeacademy on and off since 2018, but when I was offered the position within the development team I ramped up my learning to 3 day a week.
